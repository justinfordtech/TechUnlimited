Facebook Refusing To Notify Leaked Users

by justin fordapril 9th, 2021

Over 530 million Facebook users (including Zuckerberg, himself) have been breached and personal information has been leaked on a hacking forum, according to multiple sources. The leaked information includes a user’s mobile
number, Facebook ID, name, gender, location, relationship status, occupation, date of birth, email addresses and more. Despite the seriousness of the leak, Facebook has decided not to notify the individuals who were affected.

According to Reuters.com, Facebook believes the information leaked is “old” and not a threat. In a statement to Bleepingcomputer.com a Facebook spokesperson said, "This is old data that was previously reported on in 2019. We found and fixed this issue in August 2019…" While the data
leaked may be from 2019, it is hardly “old”. Many people’s phone numbers, email addresses, etc. remain the same for many years.
